    57) ! Purpose: 
    58) ! Solar radiation code
    59) ! 
    60) ! Method: 
    61) ! mji/rrtmg
    62) ! RRTMG, two-stream, with McICA
    63) ! 
    64) ! Divides solar spectrum into 14 intervals from 0.2-12.2 micro-meters.
    65) ! solar flux fractions specified for each interval. allows for
    66) ! seasonally and diurnally varying solar input.  Includes molecular,
    67) ! cloud, aerosol, and surface scattering, along with h2o,o3,co2,o2,cloud, 
    68) ! and surface absorption. Computes delta-eddington reflections and
    69) ! transmissions assuming homogeneously mixed layers. Adds the layers 
    70) ! assuming scattering between layers to be isotropic, and distinguishes 
    71) ! direct solar beam from scattered radiation.
    72) ! 
    73) ! Longitude loops are broken into 1 or 2 sections, so that only daylight
    74) ! (i.e. coszrs > 0) computations are done.
    75) ! 
    76) ! Note that an extra layer above the model top layer is added.
    77) ! 
    78) ! mks units are used.
    79) ! 
    80) ! Special diagnostic calculation of the clear sky surface and total column
    81) ! absorbed flux is also done for cloud forcing diagnostics.
    82) !
